Law Careers and Courses

Why Choose Law?

Law is more than courtroom battles—it's about justice, fairness, and supporting society. If you're passionate about logic, argument, and making change, law is an excellent path after Arts.

Popular Law Courses After Arts

Courses You Can Pursue:

  • BA LLB (5-year integrated program)
  • LLB (3-year course after graduation)
  • BBA LLB (for those interested in corporate law)
  • Diplomas in Cyber Law, IPR, etc.

Categories of Professions in Law

Career Options:

  • Corporate Lawyer
  • Criminal Lawyer
  • Human Rights Advocate
  • Legal Consultant

Corporate Lawyer

Handle business law—contracts, mergers, compliance, and more. Corporate lawyers ensure companies operate legally and efficiently.

Popular Courses:

  • BBA LLB
  • BA LLB (Corporate Law)
  • Diploma in Corporate Law

Benefits:

  • Work with top firms, MNCs, or startups
  • High earning potential
  • Be part of high-profile business deals
  • Requires specialization and internships

Criminal Lawyer

Defend those accused of crimes and ensure justice is served. This field is impactful and highly respected.

Popular Courses:

  • BA LLB
  • LLB
  • Certificate in Criminal Law

Benefits:

  • Respected profession
  • Work on challenging, meaningful cases
  • Good income potential
  • Requires deep knowledge of criminal law

Human Rights Advocate

Work with NGOs, governments, and international bodies to defend human rights and dignity.

Popular Courses:

  • BA LLB (Human Rights)
  • LLM in Human Rights Law
  • Diploma or Certificate in Human Rights

Benefits:

  • Make a social impact
  • Work with organizations like the UN
  • Travel and global exposure
  • Strong passion for justice required

Legal Consultant

Provide legal advice in areas like tax, real estate, and contracts—without going to court.

Popular Courses:

  • BA LLB
  • LLB + Diploma (Tax Law, Business Law)

Benefits:

  • Flexible work environment
  • Independent or firm-based practice
  • High demand in business sectors
  • Requires expertise and research skills
