Your Guide to Careers in the Arts

Introduction to Arts Stream

The Arts stream offers students a unique opportunity to explore a diverse range of subjects, from literature and history to sociology, political science, and more. It provides a deep understanding of human culture, behavior, and creativity. Whether you're interested in pursuing a career in law, journalism, or education, the Arts stream provides endless possibilities to make an impact in society.

Popular Courses in Arts

Law

Pursue an LLB degree and specialize in various fields such as criminal law, corporate law, and civil law.

Journalism

Get a degree in Journalism or Mass Communication and pursue a career in media, reporting, and content creation.

Fine Arts

Learn the basics of visual arts, painting, sculpture, and other creative disciplines to pursue a career in the arts.
