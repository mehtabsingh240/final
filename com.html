Your Guide to Careers in Commerce

Introduction to Commerce Stream

The Commerce stream offers a wide range of opportunities in business, finance, economics, and accounting. It builds skills in management, entrepreneurship, and corporate knowledge to thrive in various industries.

Career Options After Commerce

  • Chartered Accountancy (CA)

    Experts in financial management, auditing, and taxation. Opens doors in top firms and financial institutions.

    • Tax Consultant
    • Auditor
    • Financial Analyst
    • Corporate Accountant

    Learn more

  • Business Administration (BBA/MBA)

    For aspiring leaders in corporations or entrepreneurs, this degree develops key business and strategy skills.

    • Management Consultant
    • HR Manager
    • Marketing Manager
    • Project Manager

    Learn more

  • Finance and Banking

    Careers in investment banking, financial planning, and credit analysis. Great for number-savvy individuals.

    • Investment Banker
    • Financial Planner
    • Credit Analyst
    • Risk Manager

  • Economics and Policy

    • Startup Founder
